Architecture

Layer 1: Schema (Deterministic)

JsonLogic expressions + external functions. Machine-executable without LLM. ~80% of rules.

Layer 2: Knowledge (Semantic)

Citations, interpretation guidance, and semantic context from GHG Protocol for LLM agents.
